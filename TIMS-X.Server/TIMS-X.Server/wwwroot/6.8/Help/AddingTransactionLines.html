    Adding Transaction Lines

    Once the Appointment has been added and attended, the Transaction Lines that apply to the appointment can be added. Follow the steps outlined below:

    Add Single Transaction Line

    1. Click on the Patient's Appointment so that the appointment details are visible.
    2. Click  the Add button  at the bottom of the screen.
    3. The Claim Transaction screen will popup.

    1. Default information about the transaction will appear and can now be changed. For field definitions, refer to Transactions Overview.
    2. The From and To dates will default to the date of the associated Appointment.  Both dates can be changed by clicking on the calendar icon and choosing the correct date that the transaction occurred on.
    3. The Place of Service will default to "11-Office" and can be changed by clicking on the field.  A  list will appear, showing all the available codes. Click on the desired code and it will fill in to the field.

    1. Check the EMG box if the service was performed due to an Emergency.
    2. To filter the list of CPT codes, check one of the boxes next to CPT Selection. Or leave the All box checked to see all available codes.

    1. To enter the CPT Code, click on the empty field.  A list will appear, showing all the available codes.  Click on the desired code and it will fill in to the field.

    1. To add modifier(s) for this procedure, click on the first Modifier field and a list will show the available codes. 

    Click on the desired code to insert it into the transaction line. Additional modifiers (up to 4) can be added by clicking on the other modifier fields.   Modifiers may or may not be required, depending on the procedure performed.

    1. If desired, enter additional text in the CPT Additional Description field.  This information will be sent with the electronic claim.
    2. Add Diagnosis Code(s) by clicking on the first Diagnosis Code field. 

    A list will appear, showing all the available codes.  

    Click on the desired code and it will fill in to the field.  At least one Diagnosis Code is required.  Add additional codes by repeating the procedure on the additional fields.  Note: You can apply a maximum of 4 Diagnosis codes per transaction lines and per claim. If additional diagnosis codes are needed, create another appointment.

      1. On subsequent transaction lines, the Diagnosis code(s) will default to the previous line's codes, but can be changed if necessary.  
    1. The Unit Price will default to the price entered for the CPT Code in the Setup link. To change the price for this transaction only, click in the field and enter the new price.  Then either press the Enter key or click into another field to accept the change.  The Amount field will update automatically, by multiplying the new Rate by the Units.
    2. The Units field will default to 1.  To change the units double-click in the field and enter the new amount.  Then either press the Enter key or click into another field to accept the change.  The Charges field will update automatically, by multiplying the Rate by the new Units.
    3. To view information about the patient's previous claims and diagnosis codes, click the Previous Claims button .

    1. Click the Additional Info button to enter notes that will be included with the electronic claim or are for internal office use only.  If the transaction has previously been submitted, you can also view previous payment and adjustment information here. 

    1. Click the OK button when finished, or Cancel to abandon changes.
    2. Repeat the above procedures to add additional Transaction lines.  Up to 99 lines can be included in one claim.
    3. When all lines have been added, click the Save button, or Cancel to abandon all changes. If you do not save, and click on a different patient or link, a popup will ask if you want to save your changes.

    Add Multiple Transaction Lines at Once

    1. New in Version 6.07 - you can now quickly add multiple new transaction lines by clicking the Group Add button .
      1. The multi-item selection window will popup. 

    The top 15 most used CPT and Diagnosis codes will be at the top of the lists, with an asterisk (*) in front of the name. Simply click the codes to be used for the visit. You can also search by either the code or description by typing in the search box.

    At least 1, and no more than 4 Diagnosis codes may be selected.  Click a line again to un-select it.   Click OK to save the claim group or Cancel to abandon changes. 

    1. The lines will be added to the Transaction List, along with the corresponding diagnosis code(s).  If additional details need to be added to the lines, such as a price change or modifier added, double-click or highlight the line and click the Edit button .
    2. When all lines have been added, click the Save button, or Cancel to abandon all changes. If you do not save, and click on a different patient or link, a popup message will ask if you want to save your changes.
