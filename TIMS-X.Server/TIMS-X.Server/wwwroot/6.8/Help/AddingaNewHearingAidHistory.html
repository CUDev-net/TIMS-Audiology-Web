    Adding a New Hearing Aid History

    A Hearing Aid History can be for a Custom Ordered device, a Stock device, or to indicate a Purchased Elsewhere (Previously Owned) device. 

    Add a New Hearing Aid History


    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient (searching first if necessary).
    2. Once you have chosen the patient, click on the Add New icon . The Create New popup appears.

             

    Choose which type of device history you want to create by clicking the Hearing Aid, Earmold or Accessory button. Choosing Cancel will close the screen with no action taken.

    1. For hearing aids or earmolds choose which Side you want to create by clicking the Right or Left buttons.

    1. Select the type of history - New Order, Stock, or Purchased Elsewhere. 

    1. New Order Hearing Aid History: a blank Order screen will appear.

      1. Manufacturer, Model and Expected In date are required fields and highlighted in yellow. Only active models marked as Available for Sale will be shown.     
      2. Ordered date defaults to current date but can be changed as needed.
      3. P.O. No. is auto created by TIMS when the record is saved and will sync to QuickBooks.
      4. Enter the Vendor's Invoice and Tracking Numbers if available.
      5. Order information can be scanned into the hearing aid records and will be saved in the imaging center under the patient name. Click the Doc Scan button to open the Imaging system. Previously scanned/attached documents will be listed in the Documents section.

      1. Notes can be added in the notes field.
      2. Click OK to save order and the Hearing Aid Details screen will automatically open.
      3. See Orders for more details.
    1. Stock Item Hearing Aid History: the Stock Item selection tree will popup. 

      1. A list of available stock hearing aids for the current site will be listed on the tree. Only stock items with a stock status of "New" or "Used" will be available. The Style, Serial Number, Status, Date Received, and Price will be shown.  Hold the mouse cursor over the model to see a tooltip of the options attached.  Double-click to select the specific model you want.

      1. The Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number, Price, and Options will be automatically copied to the HA History fields.  The manufacturer and model fields will then become non-editable.
      2. The Status will automatically be set to "In Use".
      3. Fill in any other available information. The Invoice box will be checked automatically. Un-check if you do not wish to create an invoice. (see Automated Invoicing for more information)  For information on the definition of a specific field, refer to the overview section of Hearing Aid History.
      4. Click OK to save.
      5. See Stock Items for more details.
    1. Purchased Elsewhere Hearing Aid History: the Hearing Aid History details screen will popup.
      1. Select a Manufacturer and Model.  Only active models will be shown. 
      2. The Status will automatically be set to "Purchased Elsewhere".
      3. Fill in any available information. For information on the definition of a specific field, refer to the overview section of Hearing Aid History.  
      4. The Invoice box will be not be checked by default.
      5. Click OK to save.


    Earmold History

    • Follow the instructions above for either a Custom Order, Stock Item, or Purchased Elsewhere device.


    Accessory History  

    • Follow the instructions above for either a Custom Order, Stock Item, or Purchased Elsewhere device.
    • Note: There are no sides(ears) for an Accessory - that step will be skipped.


    Binaural Hearing Aids

    A binaural hearing aid or earmold fitting can be created several ways.

    1. After creating the Hearing Aid History in one of the 3 ways above, Click the Create Binaural button to add a hearing aid record for the other ear.
      1. Select the type of the other side history - New Order, Stock, or Purchased Elsewhere.
      2. Follow the instructions above for the type chosen. If the the new side is a New Order or Purchased Elsewhere, the Manufacturer and Model will be copied over (but can be changed if needed).
    2. To combine two already existing hearing aid records into a binaural fitting, click on the Link HA Records icon on either side.
      1. A confirmation message will popup. Click Yes to proceed, No to Cancel.

      1. A list of other side histories available to be linked will popup.  Select the one you want to link, then click OK.

      1. The two hearing aid histories are now linked.  

     

    Unlink Hearing Aid Histories

    The sides of a binaural fitting can be un-linked only if one or both sides have a Status that is not active. (Not Used, Lost, Void, etc). If that is the case, the Unlink icon will be available.

    When you click the Unlink icon, a confirmation message will popup. Click Yes to Unlink, No to Cancel.


    Copying Information from Either Side

    Click on the Copy To Left or Copy To Right buttons to copy information from one binaural hearing aid record to another without having to re-enter the same information. If you’re entering something unique to that side, such as a note that applies to the right side only, do not click the Copy To button.
