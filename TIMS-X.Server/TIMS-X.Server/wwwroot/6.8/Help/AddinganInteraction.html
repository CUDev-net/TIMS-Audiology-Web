    Once you have produced the desired patient list, you're ready to record interactions with the patients.  You will record who you talked to, the results of the call, and any additional notes.

    Patients Call List

    There will be an arrow next to the patient's name, if that patient has had previous interactions recorded.  Click on the arrow to expand the tree and display the previous interaction(s) date and time.

           

    If a patient's name is in red, this means that patient has had Communications Restrictions entered.  Check the Patient Summary panel to see what they are before calling.

    Add a New Interaction

    1. Select the patient name and click the Add New Interaction icon .
    2. Select a choice from the Talked To and Call Result fields.  These are required fields. Available choices are maintained in Setup.

    1. Enter any applicable notes about the interaction, then click the Save icon .
    2. The interaction will be saved, and a new entry will appear on the tree underneath the patient name.  The criteria used to produce the patient list will appear in the Parameters field.  This is not editable.  Also saved will be the date the interaction was created and last updated, and the user who last updated it.

     

    1. To schedule an appointment for the patient, click the Schedule Appointment icon . You will be taken to the Appointments link.  Schedule the appointment, then click the Back button to return to the Communications page where you left off.

    1. The interaction will appear in the Patient Activity Panel under the heading "Communication Interactions".
