    Appointment Details


    The Appointment Details screen displays all the information about the appointment, when accessed from the appointment grid or Patients List Panel.  Below are descriptions of all the appointment fields.


    Name

    The last name, first name, and middle initial of the patient as entered in the Patient details. If the patient has any Communication Restrictions, the name will

    appear in red.

    Phone

    The primary phone number of the patient, as entered in the Patient details. 

    History

    Click the History button to see a detailed list of all past and future appointments for the patient. This list can be filtered by date and/or printed.

    Opportunity

    The Opportunity Status of the patient as of the date and time the appointment was created. The status is based on a combination of factors from hearing aid history, NOAH test results, and communication restrictions and shows the potential for future hearing aid sales.  See Opportunity Tracking for more details. This field cannot be edited. If certain conditions have occurred since the appointment was originally made, such as a hearing aid purchase, return, etc., the status will be updated up until the appointment has occurred. 

    Provider

    The provider with whom the appointment has been scheduled. Required. Click on the down arrow to change, if needed.

    Appt Type

    The type of appointment the patient has been scheduled for. Required by default, but that can be changed if needed by the practice. Click on the down arrow to change types, if needed. Appointment types are added in Setup.

    Site

    The location where the patient appointment is taking place.  Required. 

    Resource

    The resource (room) where the appointment is taking place. 

    Status

    The current status of the appointment. Required. By default, newly created appointments will have a status of "New".  Click on the down arrow to change, if needed. Appointment statuses are added in Setup.

    Marketing

    The reason the patient made an appointment for that day.  Click the ellipsis button to see a popup of all the available choices. 

       

    Click the arrow symbol next to the desired category, then double-click on the source to select.  Marketing categories and sources can be added in the Setup link. 


    If this is the first appointment for the patient and a marketing source was set in

    the Patient details, that source will default here.  It can be changed if needed.


    A marketing source may be required if the practice has determined to set that rule. In addition, the practice may choose to lock the marketing source once any invoices have been associated with it - so that no future changes can be made.

    Authorization

    If appointment authorizations have been enabled, and the patient has active authorizations available, this field will display the name and number remaining. 

       

    If the authorization is selected, the number used count will increase by one. For a recurring appointment series, one authorization will be applied to each member until all have been used, or the expiration date has been reached.  Authorizations will become inactive when number of appointments has been reached or expiration date has expired. Inactive authorizations will no longer be displayed.

    Date

    The date that the appointment is scheduled for.  This date will automatically be filled out when the patient’s name has been dropped onto the Appointment Grid. The date can be changed by clicking on the calendar icon and selecting a new date.

       

    Time

    The time the appointment is scheduled to start and end.  This time will automatically

    be filled out when the patient’s name has been dropped onto the Appointment Grid. The start time can be changed by clicking on the time and typing a new value. The end time will be calculated automatically by adding the duration to the start time.   

    Duration

    The time length the appointment has been scheduled for.  This will automatically

    be set to the default duration setup for the Appointment Type that has been selected. It can be changed as needed by clicking on the arrows or typing the desired minutes and hours.  You can enter any value from 1 minute to 11 hours and 59 minutes.

    Next Contact

    The date the patient is to be contacted next. This field can be used to generate

    recall mailings.

    Take Photo

    If the patient's picture was previously taken and saved into the patient record, it will be displayed here.  If not, you can take one here by clicking this button. See Taking a Patient Picture for details.

    Scan Document

    Click here to scan or upload a document to associate with the patient's record.  See Attaching Documents to a Patient Record for details.

    Print Intake Sheet

    Click here to quickly print a patient Intake Sheet for the selected appointment.  This can be the stock Intake Sheet, or a custom one made for your practice.

    Digital Intake Sheet

    Click here to generate a message to the patient (Email and/or Text).  

    The message will contain a link for the patient to click on.  This will take them to the online digital intake form where they can submit their demographic information. See Digital Intake Form for more information.

    Add to Cancellation List

    If the patient would like to have an appointment earlier than the one they are scheduled for, click this box. This puts them on the "Cancellation List".  If a cancellation occurs, their name will be included in a popup list. They can then be quickly rescheduled to the new time slot.

    Recurrence

    If the appointment has been designated as a recurring one, this box will be checked and the details will appear in the Recurrence tab below.  This information cannot be changed after the appointment has been created.

    See Recurring Appointments for details.

    Notes

    Free form field for notes about the patient or the appointment.  If the patient has any Communication Restrictions, those restrictions will automatically be pasted into the Notes when a new appointment is made.  Up to 255 characters can be entered.

    Messages

    If the office is using Appointment Notifications, any text (SMS) messages pertaining to this appointment will be displayed in this tab.

    Tracking


       Created

    The date when the Appointment information was originally created, along with the Site and User who created it. It cannot be edited.

       Updated

    This date and User name is automatically updated when the record has been changed and saved.  It cannot be edited.
