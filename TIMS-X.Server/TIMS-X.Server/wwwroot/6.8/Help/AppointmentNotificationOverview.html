    Appointment Notification Overview

    • Contact TIMS Support at 800-763-0308 or audiologysupport@cu.net to enroll in the Automated Appointment Notification service. (fees applicable)
    • Automated Appointment Notifications can be sent as Verifications or Confirmations.
      • Verifications are sent to confirm that an appointment has been scheduled.
      • Confirmations are sent to remind a patient of an upcoming scheduled appointment and will request a response.
    • An Automated Appointment Notification can be sent to a patient as Phone Call (Voice), E-mail, or Sms (Text).
      • Voice
        • The Practice phone number will be displayed on the Caller ID when a patient receives a Voice Notification phone call.
        • The phone number for the patient must be in a 10 digit format to be recognized by the Notification system.
        • Voice Notifications are sent to the home phone number in the patient file.  If the home phone number is blank, the notification is sent to the patient's mobile number.
        • Voice Notification Confirmations will ask the patient to respond with 1 to confirm appointment; 2 to reschedule; and 3 to cancel the appointment.
        • A Voice message is comprised of typed text and merge fields incorporating specific information relating to the patient such as appointment dates and times.
      • Email
        • The origination email address can be specified when Appointment Notifications are turned on.
        • An Automated Appointment Notification E-mail is sent to the E-mail address in the patient file.
        • Email Confirmations will ask the patient the option to respond by clicking buttons to confirm appointment, reschedule, or cancel the appointment.
        • An email message is comprised of typed text and merge fields incorporating specific information relating to the patient such as appointment dates and times.
      • Text
        • The Practice phone number will be displayed on the Caller ID when a patient receives a Text Confirmation or Verification.
        • An Automated Appointment Notification Text is sent to the mobile phone number in the patient file.
        • The phone number for the patient must be in a 10 digit format to be recognized by the Notification system.
        • Text Confirmations will ask the patient  to respond with 1 to confirm appointment; 2 to reschedule; and 3 to cancel the appointment.
        • A text message is comprised of typed text and merge fields incorporating specific information relating to the patient such as appointment dates and times.
    • Any combination of the three types of Automated Appointment Notifications can be sent to the same patient.
    • It is recommended that Automated Appointment Notifications be sent as a single batch once a day.


    Assign Permission to Send Automated Appointment Notifications


    • It is recommended that only one User be responsible for sending Automated Appointment Notifications.
    • Permission to send Automated Appointment Notifications is assigned to a specific User in User Group Setup.
