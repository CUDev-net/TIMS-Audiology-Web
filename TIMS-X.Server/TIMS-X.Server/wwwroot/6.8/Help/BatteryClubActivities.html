    Battery Club Activities

    Deluxe version Battery Club disbursements can be performed for a group of patients in the Battery Center or individually, by opening the Battery Club screen for the selected patient.  Adding to balance and closing the Battery Club must be done individually.


    Add to Balance (Deluxe version only) 

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.

    1. Click the Edit Setup button .
    2. Increase the number of batteries to the desired amount in Battery Balance by typing the new amount or using the arrows.

    1. Click the Save, or the Cancel button to discard your changes. 
    2. The Balance will be increased by the number of batteries added, and display as an "Add" action in the Activity List.


    Pickup for One Patient

    Standard Version:

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.

    1. The Date will default to the next scheduled Action Date, based on the Actions per Year originally entered when setup.  This can be changed as needed.
    2. The Batteries quantity will default to the number entered per action when the club was first setup.  This can be changed to any amount needed.
    3. Click Process Action button to dispense, or Close to cancel.
    4. If your club is setup as Standard w/ Invoicing, a message will ask if you'd like to invoice the action or not. Clicking Yes will automatically create a POS Invoice for the quantity of batteries dispensed.

    1. After processing the Action, click Close.


    Deluxe Version:

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.

    1. If the Delivery Method has previously been setup as "Pickup", then the Method showing in the Action section will show that as well. 

    1. Click the Process Action button to record the pickup.
    2. If needed, you can change the Action Date, Delivery Method, or number of Batteries for this action only. Edit the desired field, then click Process Action.  If the Action Date is changed, a message will popup asking if you want to recalculate future Action Dates.

    1. After processing the Action, click Close.


    Mail Out to One Patient (Deluxe version only)

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.
    3. If the Delivery Method has previously been setup as "Mail Out", then the Method showing in the Action section will show that as well. 

    1. Click the Process button to record the mailing.
    2. If needed, you can change the Action Date, Delivery Method, or number of Batteries for this action only. Edit the desired field, then click Process Action.  If the Action Date is changed, a message will popup asking if you want to recalculate future Action Dates.

    1. After processing the Action, click Close.

    Close Out Battery Club

    Standard Version:

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.
    3. Click the Close Account button .
    4. A confirmation message will popup. Click OK to continue, or No to cancel.

    1. The Battery Club is now closed for this patient and no more activity can be recorded. Note that Closing is final - you can not void a Close action.
    2. The Battery Club line in the Patient Activity tree will indicate that it is closed.


    Deluxe Version:

    1. Click on the Patient Tree and choose the desired patient.
    2. Click the Battery Club button in either the Hearing Aid History summary or the Patient Summary Panel.  The Battery Club Screen will popup.
    3. Click the Close Battery Club button .
    4. If the remaining balance is greater than zero, a warning message will popup.  Click Yes to continue, or No to cancel.  

    1. The Battery Club is now closed for this patient and no more activity can be recorded. Note that Closing is final - you can not void a Close action.
    2. The Battery Club line in the Patient Activity tree will indicate that it is closed. You will still be able to view past actions by double-clicking on the list.

    1. If the club has been closed with remaining batteries that have been previously invoiced, you will be prompted to create a credit memo in the POS module for the remaining batteries.
