    Battery Club Overview

    Battery Club is used to insure a patient will always have the batteries needed for their aid to operate. The patient’s totals will be tracked and several reports are available for tracking this information.  The details of a patient's Battery Club can be seen in the Battery Club screen, which can be accessed from the Patient Summary Panel and Hearing Aid History summary.  Click on the Battery Club button to open the Battery Club screen. If there is an open battery club for the selected hearing aid, this button will be orange . Otherwise, it will be blue . Battery Club is available for Hearing Aids only.

    Deluxe Screen


    The Battery Club Activity List can be accessed from the patient's Activity Panel. 

    Double-click on the line to see a list of all actions taken.


    Battery Club Versions

    There are 3 different versions of the Battery Club.  Choose the one that best fits your practice's needs. Contact TIMS Support to set the version needed.


    1. Standard - This is the simplest version, tracking only how many batteries were dispensed, when they were dispensed, and the expiration date.
    2. Standard with Invoicing - This has the same functionality as the Standard version above, but with the ability to invoice the batteries dispensed.
    3. Deluxe  - This version has the most features and tracking, and works in conjunction with the Battery Center. Batteries can be designated as either Mailed Out or Picked Up.  A specific number of batteries is allocated when the club is setup, along with how and if they are invoiced.  Expiration date and balance are tracked.


    • A new Battery Club must be set up in HA History.
    • An established Battery Club can be viewed and edited from the patient summary panel or the HA History record.
    • Battery Club batteries can be dispensed for multiple patients from the Battery Center. (Deluxe version only)


    Battery Club Details

    Setup

    Standard

    Patient Name

    The name of the patient as shown on the patient tree. This field is automatically updated when the patient name is highlighted.

    Hearing Aid

    The Model and Ear of the associated Hearing aid will be displayed on the top line.  This cannot be changed.

    Battery Club Account

    A patient may have a more than one battery club account if he or she uses multiple sizes of batteries.  Select the account you wish to work with in this dropdown field.

    Close Account

    Click this button if you wish to close the displayed Battery Club.  A confirmation message will popup.

       

    Expiration Date

    Enter the date the battery club will expire.

    Batteries per Action

    The number of batteries that the patient is  to receive each period.

    Actions Per Year

    The frequency of battery disbursements for this club.  If monthly, choose 12.

    Notes

    A free form field for notes. Up to 255 characters can be entered.


    Deluxe

    Patient Name

    The name of the patient as shown on the patient tree. This field is automatically updated when the patient name is highlighted.

    Hearing Aid

    The Model and Ear of the associated Hearing aid will be displayed on the top line.  This cannot be changed.

    Battery Club Account

    A patient may have a more than one battery club account if he or she uses multiple sizes of batteries.  Select the account you wish to work with in this dropdown field.

    Close Battery Club

    Click this button if you wish to close the displayed Battery Club.  A confirmation message will popup if there are remaining batteries left.

       

    Battery Size

    Battery size of the associated Hearing aid.  This cannot be changed.

    Invoice Method

    If the entire cost of the battery club is to be billed at once on one invoice, choose "Beginning Balance".  If each battery disbursement will be billed on a separate invoice, choose "Per Action".  If the practice allows Promo (non-billable) battery clubs -then choose "Promo".

    Delivery Method

    Choose whether the patient will be picking up the batteries or have them mailed out.

    Battery Balance

    The field used to keep a running balance of the patient’s batteries. When batteries are picked up or mailed out, the number received will be subtracted automatically from the balance. 

    Batteries per Action

    The number of batteries that the patient is  to receive each period.

    Action Interval

    The frequency of battery disbursements for this club. 

       

    Initial Action Date

    The date a patient starts the Battery Club. Automatically displays today’s date when a new account  is added.

    Final Action Date

    Automatically calculated based on the Initial Action Date, number of batteries and actions per year.

    Notes

    A free form field for notes. Up to 255 characters can be entered.

    Edit Setup

    Once the club has been setup, click this button to change the Delivery Method, Balance, Batteries per Action, and/or Action Interval.  Other settings can not be edited.
