    CareCredit® Payments

    Financing Icon

    Click on the Financing icon in the patient summary panel, then click the CareCredit button for CareCredit® application options.

      

    Electronic Application

    • Click the Apply for Credit (Web) button to enter patient information into the electronic application. 

    • You will be taken to the CareCredit website.  Complete the application there.


    Lookup Account

    • Click the Lookup Account button to look up an established account.

    • Enter the patient social security number and zip code to access account information.

    • Click Transmit button and the results from CareCredit  will be displayed.


    CareCredit Payment on Invoice

    • Click on the patient point of sale icon in the patient summary panel.

    • Choose invoice option.
    • Create a new invoice or open an existing invoice.
    • Click the radio button in front of the CareCredit option.

    • Add a new line on the invoice.
    • Select Type payment using drop down options.
    • Select Item CareCredit using drop down options.

    • Enter Unit Price.
    • Amount will be automatically entered when you click OK button.
    • CareCredit payment line will be displayed on invoice.


    CareCredit Payment without Invoice

    • Click on the patient point of sale icon in the patient summary panel.

    • Choose payment option.
    • Create a new payment or open an existing payment. 
    • Click the radio button in front of the CareCredit option.

    • Choose payment method CareCredit from the drop down options. 

    • Enter payment amount.
