    CareCredit™ Payments

    Once a patient has an establish CareCredit™ account, either by applying through TIMS or already existing, payments can be made through the POS System.  A CareCredit payment (purchase) can be made as a line item on an invoice or as a separate payment document.

    CareCredit Payment on Invoice

    1. Select the desired patient in the Patients Panel, then click on the point of sale icon. 

    1. Click the Invoice button. Create a new invoice or open an existing one. (see POS Invoice help for more details)
    2. Click the radio button in front of the CareCredit option to indicate that this invoice will contain a CareCredit payment line.

    1. Add line items for items and services.
    2. When all charges have been entered, add a Payment line.
      1. Select "Payment" for Type.
      2. Select "Pmt-CareCredit" for Item.

      1. Enter the Amount of the CareCredit payment for Payment Amount.
      2. Amount will be automatically entered.
    1. The CareCredit payment line will be displayed on the invoice.

    1. Note: the payment request will not be submitted to CareCredit until the Invoice is finalized. If you are not ready to finalize the invoice yet, you can close it at this point and come back later. (see Saving POS Documents)
    2. To Finalize the invoice and submit the payment request to CareCredit:
      1. Click the Final button . A confirmation message will popup - click Yes to continue.
      2. The CareCredit Purchase screen will popup. 

      1. If you have previously looked up this patient's CareCredit account, or applied for a new account from TIMS, their existing Account Number will be pre-filled.  If it is not filled and this number is available, you can type it in here. If they have a CareCredit credit card and you have an attached card swiping device, click Swipe Card to obtain the account information.

      1. The Purchase Amount will be pre-filled in with the amount previously entered on on the payment line.
      2. A Promo Code must be selected. To get a list of the available codes for this purchase, click the Lookup button.

      1. When the progress window closes, available codes will be shown in the Promo Code drop-down list.  Choose the desired promo.

      1. Once the Promo Code is selected, hover the mouse cursor over the field to see a tool tip with details about the promotion terms. Read the promotional financing disclosure to the customer for the promotion the customer selected.

      1. Enter a description of the sale in the in Order Description field if desired.
      2. When all information has been entered, click the Transmit button to submit the payment request.
    1. If the purchase is approved, a message indicating this will popup.  Click OK to continue.

      1. A receipt will automatically be sent to default printer. Accept the printer selected or select a different one, then click Print. Two copies of the "Sales Draft" receipt will print.

    1. If the transaction is denied, as message will popup indicating CareCredit's response.  You may need to contact CareCredit to resolve the issue. If the payment does not process at CareCredit, you will see this message and the invoice cannot be finalized. You must resolve the issue with CareCredit or change the payment type to something else. Click OK.

    1. Click the black Close button to return to the POS Invoice screen, then click Exit.


    CareCredit Payment without Invoice

    1. Click the Payment/Refund Check button. Create a new payment or open an existing one. (see POS Payment Receipt help for more details)
    2. Click the radio button in front of the CareCredit option to indicate that this payment is from CareCredit.

    1. Select "CareCredit" for Payment Method and enter the Amount of the payment. The Payment Reference will be filled in automatically after a response is received from CareCredit.
