    Claims Reports

    Analytics/Reporting - Data Analysis

    Outstanding Claims - a summary showing outstanding claims grouped and totaled by aging and status.  All or any sites can be selected. Switch the Report View to "Aged Receivables Chart" to display the Aged Receivables report for that aging period, which will show outstanding totals by Payer or CPT Code.  Switch view to "Outstanding Claims Detail"  to see details of the individual claims.  (see example)  Multiple parameters are available to filter the view to see specific claim categories.

    Analytics/Reporting - TIMS Reports - Claims

    1. Claim Payments by Payer - Claim payments by payer, either as primary or secondary, for the selected DOS or payment date range.  Optional Site and Payer parameter.  Grouped by Site, Payer, then CPT Code.
    2. Claims by Insurance Coverage - Claims with a DOS in the selected date range, which contain Payers of the selected type and optional name.  Totaled by number of claims and patients.
    3. Completed Claims Not Synced - Lists all completed transactions, payments, and adjustments that have not been synced to QuickBooks.
    4. Completed Claims with Balance - Detail list of all completed claims that still have a balance.
    5. Insurance Coverage - List of patients covered by insurance, sorted by payer.  Choose type of coverage, primary through quinary.  Includes total number of patients covered.
    6. Open Item - All open claim transactions (generated into a claim or not), summarized by patient.
    7. Payments by CPT Code - Claim transactions in selected date range, grouped by CPT Code.  Shows amounts billed, paid by insurance and patient, and average amount received and allowable per code.  Drill down to see transaction details.
    8. Response Files by Date Range - A report sorted by date, that displays all clearinghouse response files. Used for reference and to obtain information that may need to be manually entered on the Adjudication Detail link, or for review.
    9. Transaction Journal - All Patients - A list of claim transactions for all patients for the selected period.
    10. Transmitted Claims by Date Range - A list of all claims transmitted in the selected date range. Includes payer name and total charges.
