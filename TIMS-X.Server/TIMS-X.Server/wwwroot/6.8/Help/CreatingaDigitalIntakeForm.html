    Creating a Digital Intake Form

    1. Open a browser and go to us.timsaudiology.com  Enter your office code (issued by TIMS Audiology).

    1. Enter your Username and password.

    1. Click the Patient Form Editor link:

    1. Click in the Select Form drop down to select Patient Intake.  Click the Edit Form button.

    1. Initially, all fields will be on the left.

    1. These arrows allow the selected field(s) to be moved the right, to show on the Digital Intake. 

    1. This arrow allows one to add all fields to the Digital Intake. 

    1. Once the Current Form contains all desired fields, they can be moved to different areas of the Digital Intake, but clicking and dragging the dotted field holders.

    1. Additional fields can also be added, by clicking Add New, and selecting one of the available types. When dragged to the right, it will default to the bottom of the form.

     

    1. To edit the name of a field, click the Edit button.

    1. When edits are complete, click Save.
