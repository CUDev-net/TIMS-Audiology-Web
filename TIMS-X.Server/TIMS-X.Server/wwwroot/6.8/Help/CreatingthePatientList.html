    Creating the Patient List

    Multiple criteria can be used to create a patient Call or Letter list filtered to your needs.  

    By default, only active and non-deceased patients are included.  Choose one or more of the following criteria, then click the Build List button to generate the resulting patient list.  

    Note: If a Letter List is to be generated, you must first select a letter before clicking Build List. (not necessary for a Call List only)


    Criteria are separated by category.  Choices can be made across multiple categories. To clear out all previously selected criteria, in order to start a new search - click the Clear Criteria button . Once you've expanded the various criteria and made your selection, you can make more room on the screen to view letters and lists by clicking the Collapse All button . 


    Once you have built a patient letter list, a message will popup asking if you wish to save the criteria/parameters used.  

    If you frequently use a certain set of criteria for a particular letter list, this can be very helpful.  Once the parameters are saved, the next time the letter is selected the same criteria will be loaded.  Previously saved 

    parameters can be cleared by clicking the Reset Stored Parameters button on the Letters Panel.   Note: - parameters are saved for letter lists only, not call lists.


    These main criteria are expanded by clicking the button:

    Patient Filter Criteria

    OT Status

    Most recent Opportunity Status of patient - Re-Sell (1 or 2 ears), Tested Not Sold (1 or 2 ears), Other (1 or 2 ears aidable, or No Opportunity.  See Opportunity Tracking for definitions.

    Noah Mining Criteria

    Filter by criteria previously created in Noah Data Mining setup.

    Site

    Include only patients who have the check Site as their default.

    County

    Include only patients who have the checked Counties (if used)

    Inc Patient Type

    Include only patients who have the checked Patient Types.

    Exc Patient Type

    Exclude patients who have the checked Patient Types.

    Exc Comm Restrictions

    Exclude patients who have the checked Communication Restrictions.

    Insurance

    Include only patients who have the checked Insurance Payers (for any level, primary - quinary)

    Previous Medical History

    Include only patients who have the checked Previous History conditions.

    Patient Marketing

    Include only patients who have the checked Marketing Source(s) in Patient details.

    Language

    Include only patients who have the checked Language(s) in Patient Details.

    Patient Age

    Range of patient ages to be included.  Leave both at zero if not using.

    Zip Code

    Range of patient zip codes.

    Birthday

    Patient's Birthday Month.  For example, choose September 01 to September 30 to include all patient's with a birthday in September, regardless of age.

    w/ Last Interaction

    Include patients who have had a last communication interaction in this date range.

    wo/ Last Interaction Date

    Include patients who have no recorded communication interactions in this date range.

    Custom Date 1 and 2

    Include patients with Custom Dates 1 or 2 in this date range. The actual Custom Name as entered in Field Descriptions will be displayed here.

    Custom Text 1 and 2

    Include patients with Custom Text 1 or 2 equal to this value. The actual Custom Name as entered in Field Descriptions will be displayed here.

    Appointment Filter Criteria

    Filter Type

    Filter for Any, Last, First, or Next appointment.

    Appt Date

    Date range of patient's appointment (last, next, or any, based on filter above).

    Appt Type

    The type of the patient's appointment. (last, next, or any, based on filter above).

    Appt Status

    The status of the patient's appointment. (last, next, or any, based on filter above).

    Appt Provider

    The assigned provider for the patient's appointment. (last, next, or any, based on filter above)

    Next Contact Date

    Date range of the patient's Next Contact Date.

    Appt Marketing

    The marketing source of the patient's appointment. (last, next, or any, based on filter above)

    Future Appts

    Include patients with future appointments or no future appointments.

    History Filter Criteria

    Filter Type

    Filter on Any History Date or Last History Date only.

    History Type

    The type of the patient's History visit - either Any or Last, depending on what it chosen above.

    History Date

    Date range of patient's history visit - either Any or Last, depending on what it chosen above.

    Severity

    Severity recorded in the patient's History visit - either Any or Last, depending on what it chosen above.

    Configuration

    Configuration recorded in the patient's History visit - either Any or Last, depending on what it chosen above.

    Type of Loss

    Type of Loss recorded in the patient's History visit - either Any or Last, depending on what it chosen above.

    HA History Filter Criteria

    Filter Type

    Filter on Any HA History Date or Last HA History Date only.

    Device Type

    Choose All, Hearing Aids only, Earmolds only, or Accessories only.

    HA Manufacturer

    Manufacturer of the device.

    HA Model

    Model name(s) of the device, from the above selected Manufacturer.

    HA Style

    HA Style of the device(s) to include.

    HA Type

    HA Type of the device(s) to include.

    Hearing Aid Status

    HA Status of the device(s) to include.

    Provider

    The Seen By Provider on the HA History.

    Purchase Date

    Date range of the HA History Purchase Date.

    Warranty Type

    Warranty type of the device.

    Warranty Date

    Date range of the device's Warranty Date expiration.

    Fit Date

    Date range of the HA History Fit Date.

    L/D Warranty Type

    Loss/Damage Warranty type of the device.

    L/D Date

    Date range of the HA History Loss/Damage Date.
