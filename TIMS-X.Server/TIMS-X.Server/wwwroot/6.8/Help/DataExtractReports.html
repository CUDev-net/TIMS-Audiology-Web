    Data Extract Reports

    Data Extract exports are designed and formatted to be exported to Excel®.  From there they can be used as mail merge lists, or analyzed with other programs such as Microsoft Power BI®. They have no or minimal parameters, so that they can be filtered and/or manipulated in other software.


    1. Accounts Receivable Export - List of all AR transactions generated by Patient Statements.
    2. Appointment Export - Detail list of all appointments in the selected date range. Includes details of HA sales linked to the appointment, and marketing source.
    3. Claim Transaction Export - Detail list of all claim transactions with dates of service in the selected date range.
    4. Current Opportunities Export - List of all active, non-deceased patients with demographics. Includes current opportunity status, current HA details, last and next appointment details, patient type and communication restrictions.
    5. Devices on Loan Export - List of HA devices currently outstanding as loaners.
    6. Devices on Trial Export - List of HA devices with current status of "Trial Period".
    7. ESCO Active HA Model Export - List of all active hearing aid and accessory models.
    8. ESCO TIMS Export - List of all active, non-deceased patients who have an active hearing aid.
    9. HA History Export - Detail list of all Hearing Aid Histories for the selected Purchase or Fit date range.  Includes hearing aids, earmolds, and accessories.
    10. History Export - Detail list of all Histories in the selected date range.
    11. Patient Details Export - List of all patients with demographics, with a choice of viewing active, non-deceased only.  Includes current HA details, last and first appointment details, last history and audiogram dates, primary insurance, referring doctor, and marketing source.
    12. Sales Export - List of all sales transactions (POS and Claims) in the selected date range.  Includes marketing source.
    13. Stock on Hand Export - Detail list of stock items available for sale.
