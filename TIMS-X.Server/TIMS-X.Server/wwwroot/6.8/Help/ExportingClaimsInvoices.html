  1. QuickBooks®

    Exporting Claims Invoices

    • Claims will export as an invoice after the transaction has been processed to a claim and a response has been received by the clearinghouse. An invoice will be created after QB Sync is executed.
    • Create a transaction in TIMS, generate the batch and submit it to the clearing house. (see Generating a Claim)
    • Once the claim has been processed by the clearinghouse and received back into TIMS in Adjudication, the claim is available to be sent to QuickBooks® with QB sync and made into an invoice.
    • Several items are referenced on the invoice from the claim information. Invoices will contain the same transaction lines as the original claim. If more than one claim was submitted for the patient, the same number of invoices will be generated. The line items on the invoice will correspond to the transactions billed - the CPT code, description, date of service, and price billed will be exported.
    • The invoice date in QuickBooks will be the claim transmit date.


     

    The invoice will appear in the patient's (customer's) register, with the Memo "TIMS Claim ## submitted to "Payer" on 9/8/2021"
