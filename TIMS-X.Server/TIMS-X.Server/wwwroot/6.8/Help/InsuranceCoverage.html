    Insurance Coverage

    The Patient Insurance Editor screen contains the information about the insurance coverage of the patient. Up to five insurances can be recorded for each patient - Primary,  Secondary, Tertiary, Quaternary, and Quinary. Insurance information can be accessed by clicking the Insurance Payers button in the Patient Details, the Edit Insurance icon in the Patient Summary Panel, or the Insurance Payers button in the Claims Processing page. 

    New in version 6.07 - practices whose databases are hosted with TIMS can now upload photos of insurance cards directly from their mobile device by scanning a QR code.


    If the Patient's Relation to Insured field is "Self", the patient's demographic information will automatically be used.  Otherwise, enter the appropriate demographics for the insured person.   The tools above the insured demographic fields can speed up entry. 

    • Copy from Patient - copies all demographic information from the patient details. This can be helpful when the insured is a spouse or child and the address and phone are the same.
    • Clear All - clears all the insured fields, so that they can be re-entered.
    • Clear Partial - clears only the First Name, Birth Date and Gender fields.


    When you are finished entering the insurance information click OK to save, or Cancel to abandon changes.


    Insurance Card Photos

    View and/or upload a copy of the patient's insurance card.

    1. Click the down arrow next to Insurance Card Photos.

         

    1. Choose which payer (Primary, Secondary, etc.) to view or upload a card to.

         

    1. To manually upload an image file or scan the card, click Upload File from PC icon. 

       

    1. Choose "Insurance Card" as the Document Type, the Payer Slot, and either Front or Back. Save image.  Repeat with other side of card.
    2. To upload from a mobile device (if enabled), click the Use Mobile Device icon.

         

        Follow the steps in Mobile Uploading help.

    1. Once uploaded, click on the Front or Back window to see a larger view of the photo.

       

    1. Photos can also be viewed from the Patient Activity Panel under Patient Documents - Insurance Card.

       

    Insurance Payer

    The name of the payer organization from which the provider expects or receives some reimbursement for the claim. 

    Relationship to Patient (Insured)

    Used to indicate the relationship of the patient to the insured individual. If the relation is Self, the information from the  Patient details  will automatically be used for the Insured's demographic fields.  Use Self-Other if the patient is the subscriber, but uses a different name or other demographic information for insurance purposes.

    Insurance ID

    A unique number assigned by the payer to identify the insured. Required for all insurance payers.

    Group Name

    The name of the group or plan through which the insurance is provided to the insured individual.

    Group Number

    An identification number, control number, or code assigned by the payer or administrator used to identify the group under which the insured is covered. Usually required by commercial payers only.

    Medicare Secondary Code

    Select code from this list only if Medicare is the secondary payer.

    Carrier ID

    A code used by some carriers when being billed as a secondary payer.  This code refers to (and is entered for) the primary payer in the secondary billing situation.  It is payer-defined, and can be different from the clearinghouse defined payer ID.

    Electronic Insurance Eligibility

    The patient's current insurance coverage information for this payer can be checked in real-time if the practice has previously been enrolled for eligibility. Fees applicable - contact TIMS Support at 800-763-0308 for details. Click the Get button to contact the payer and receive the eligibility data. The Eligibility Lookup screen will popup. 

    Select the type of coverage and date of service, then click OK. TIMS will obtain the information electronically, then display the results. Click the View button to see previously obtained information. (see example of eligibility data)

    Insurance Notes

    A free form text field for entering any relevant notes about this coverage.  (not sent with claims)

    First - Initial

    The First name and Middle Initial of the insured.

    Last Name

    The Last name of the insured.

    Address1 - Zip Code

    The mailing address of the insured. 

    Phone No

    The telephone number, including area code, at which the insured may be contacted.

    Insured's Birth Date

    The date of birth of the insured individual.

    Insured's Gender

    A code used to identify the gender (sex) of the insured.

    Release Signature

    The date the insured signed a release statement allowing medical information to be shared with the insurance payer.
