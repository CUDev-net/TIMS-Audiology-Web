    Loaners

    You can record information about devices loaned to patients by clicking the Loaner button in the Hearing Aid Summary. Loaner procedures are the same for both Hearing Aids and Accessories. 

    Note: The Loaner function is not available for Earmold hearing aid histories.

    Create a Loaner

    1. Click the Loaner button in the Hearing Aid Summary.  The new Loaner screen will popup.

    1. A loaner can be created manually by selecting the Manufacturer and Model OR by selecting an existing device from Stock.
      1. Manually: Choose a Manufacturer and Model.  Date Out will default to the current date, but can be changed as needed. Select a date the Loaner is Due Back. These fields are required. Enter the serial number if known, and and Notes if desired.  
        1. The Return to Stock checkbox will be checked and the Return to Site and Stock Status fields will become visible.  This means that the device will be returned to stock when the loaner is returned.

      1. From Stock: Click the Stock Item button . The Stock Item selection tree will popup. 

        1. A list of available stock hearing aids for the current site will be listed on the tree. Only stock items with a stock status of "New" or "Used" will be available. Hold the mouse cursor over the model to see a tooltip of the options attached.  Double-click to select the specific model you want. The Manufacturer, Model, and Serial Number will be automatically copied to the loaner fields.
        2. The Stock Item button will turn orange. The Return to Stock checkbox will be checked and the Return to Site and Stock Status fields will become visible.  This means that the device will be returned to stock when the loaner is returned.


    1. Click OK to save, or Cancel to abandon changes. The Loaner button will turn orange and display "Check In". This indicates that the loaner is Outstanding.


    Return a Loaner

    1. Click the Check In button in the Hearing Aid Summary. The Loaner details screen will popup.

    1. Enter a Date In to indicate when the loaner was returned.  This is required for a check in. 
    2. All loaners are returned to stock when checked in, regardless of whether they were originally created manually or pulled from stock.
      1. Return to Site will default to the logged in site, but can be changed as needed. 
      2. Choose the Return to Stock Status that you wish the device to have when it goes back to stock.
    3. Click OK to save, or Cancel to abandon changes.The Return For Resale screen will popup.  

      1. Click Return To Stock, or type an Inactive Reason and click Inactivate Item.  The device will then be automatically transferred to Stock on Hand, with a note indicating that it is a returned Loaner. If the device originally was pulled from stock, the patient name will be included in that note.
    1. The Loaner button will turn back to blue and display "Loaner" again.   This indicates the loaner is now complete, and a new loaner can be added if needed later.


    Loaner History

    All of a patient's current and past loaner devices can be viewed by running the Loaner History (Patient) report. Click on the Reports icon in the Hearing Aid History summary.

    Under Patient, choose Loaner History (Patient).
