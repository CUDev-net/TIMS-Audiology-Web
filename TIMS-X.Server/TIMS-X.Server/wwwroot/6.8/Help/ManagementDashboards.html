    Management Dashboards

    The  Data Analysis link will show an integrated picture of your business.  

    The Management Dashboard reports will provide status of sales, marketing, claims and appointments, along with the ability to view activity details. Reports viewed in this link can be printed or exported to a PDF, Excel, or Word file. Most have a choice on how they can be viewed - Chart, Details, Labels, Export List etc.

    They can be filtered in a variety of ways to view specific data  - after selecting your filters, click the Apply button to view the filtered report.


    • Appointment Comparison - a detail view displaying the details of appointments for the selected date range. The default range is month to date.  Switch the Report View to "Graph" for a graph showing the number of appointments for the period selected, either by appointment date or create date, along side the same period for the previous year.  The graph can be separated by site if desired. Switch the Report View to "Chart" to see a summary view of appointments grouped by Site and Provider, then Type, Status, or Marketing Source. (see example)
    • HA History Summary - a detail view summarizing HA History records for the selected date range.  The default range is month to date.  All or any sites can be selected.   The report can be filtered in many ways, including which specific date: Purchase Date, Fit Date, Return Date, Warranty Date, etc.  A label view and export list is also available.  (see example)  
    • Current Opportunities - a listing of the current Opportunity Status of each active patient in the database.  A chart view, detail list and an export list of patient names and addresses can be generated. See Opportunity Tracking Reports for more information. (see example)
    • Opportunity Tracking  - a report that summarizes appointment and related hearing aid sales for the selected date range.  Can be used to track closure rates of appointments. See Opportunity Tracking Reports for more information. (see example)
    • Outstanding Claims - a summary showing outstanding claims grouped and totaled by aging and status.  All or any sites can be selected. Switch the Report View to "Aged Receivables Chart" to display the Aged Receivables report for that aging period, which will show outstanding totals by Payer or CPT Code.  Switch view to "Outstanding Claims Detail"  to see details of the individual claims.  (see example)
    • Payer Payments - a graph showing  the 10 payers with the most amount of claim charges submitted for DOS in the selected period.  The graph displays adjustments, primary payments and all other payments, which total to the charges submitted. Only completed claims with a zero balance are included. A chart below the graph displays the percentage amount versus charges for each. (see example)
    • Sales by Marketing - a graph showing sales for the period selected, grouped by marketing category, along side the same period for the previous year.  All or any sites can be selected, and the graph can be separated by site if desired.  Switch the Report View to "Sales by Category Chart" to display to the Monthly Sales by Marketing Source report, which shows the marketing category broken down by source.  Change the view to "Sales by Category Detail" to see details of the individuals sales.  (see example)
    • Sales by Source - a graph showing sales for the period selected, grouped by sales source (POS or Claims), along side the same period for the previous year.  All or any sites can be selected, and the graph can be separated by site if desired.  Switch the Report View to "Sales by Category Chart" to display to the Monthly Sales by Source report, which shows the marketing category broken down by source.  Change the view to "Sales by Category Detail" to see details of the individual sales.  (see example)
    • Business Analytics - A KPI report comparing sales versus goals for the selected fiscal year.  Graphs are shown for the entire practice and individual sites.  A site breakdown chart shows sales per category.  If a provider name is selected, a "provider report" will be displayed that shows details for that provider only. If selected, a Goal Summary will print on page one outlining the site and provider goals for the year and per month.  Any difference in the totals will be displayed. Goals must be previously entered in KPI Goals and Provider Setup.  (see example)
