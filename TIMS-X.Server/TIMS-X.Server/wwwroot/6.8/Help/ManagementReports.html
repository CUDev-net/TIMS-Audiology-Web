    Management Reports

    1. Communications Report - A list report detailing patients with Interaction  Dates in the selected period. Displays results of call, notes, and next appointment date.  (Replaces previous report Recall Report)
    2. First Visit Statistics - Used to track the effectiveness of marketing strategies by listing devices fit after a "First Visit" appointment.  Displays average number of appointments before first sale was recorded. Grouped by appointment Marketing source and Site.  Drill Down on each source to see details.
    3. Letter Follow Up - Used to track the outcome of letter campaigns. A list of patients who were sent a letter in the selected date range.  Includes optional filters for letter name, site, and follow up scheduled appointment status. Includes chart to show letter response rate and export list.
    4. Projected Future Opportunities - Displays patients with "In Use" hearing aids that will be past the threshold on the selected date.  Optional filters for hearing aid model, purchase date, severity, type of loss, patient type, previous history, and site.  View as detail report, labels or export list.
    5. Provider Claim Activity for Period - Total charges of transactions for selected period, sorted by Provider and CPT code.  
    6. Required Field Exceptions - a list of all patients who are missing the information designated as required in Practice setup.
    7. Return Visit Marketing Statistics -  A summary of return visits, grouped by Site and appointment Marketing Source. Includes a choice to filter out specific Appointment Statuses.
    8. Superbills Posted by Date - Audit report to display details of finalized superbills for the selected appointment date range.  Can be used to see if items entered on the superbill were deleted or changed, or items were double-billed.  
    9. Task Report - List of user tasks, filtered by completion status, user, type, patient status, and completion date.
    10. User Permission List - List of all TIMS Users and permissions.  Optional filters for User and/or permission.
