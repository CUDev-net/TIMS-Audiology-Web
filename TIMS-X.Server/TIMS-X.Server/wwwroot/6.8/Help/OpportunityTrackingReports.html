    Opportunity Tracking Reports

    There are two reports in the Data Analysis link that utilize the functionality of Opportunity Tracking. When TIMS features are used to their full capability, these reports will provide valuable information for your business. Access them in the Analytics/Reporting/Data Analysis link.


    Current Opportunities Report

    This report will display the Current Opportunity Status of each active and non-deceased patient in the database.  Opportunity status can  change as certain events occur (hearing aid sale, new test results, etc). Beginning in version 6.07, this status is updated automatically whenever an event takes place that will affect it. The exception is when the purchase date of an active hearing aid goes beyond the threshold set in Practice setup and becomes a "Re-sell" opportunity.  A task needs to be setup to calculate these - contact TIMS Support for assistance.    


    The report can be used as a summary chart, detail list, or exported mailing list for a specifically targeted segment of your patients. The optional filter can be used to show patients in the Re-Sell (Re-Sell 1 Ear, Re-Sell 2 Ears), Tested Not Sold (Tested Not Sold, Tested Not Sold 1 Ear), Other (1 Ear Aidable, 2 Ears Aidable) or No Opportunity categories (No Marketing, No Marketing 1 Ear, No Opportunity). (see Opportunity Tracking in Action for definitions)


    You can also filter by Site, Patient Type, Scheduled Appointment Status, Last Appointment Type and Date Range, Last History Type and Date Range, Patient Age, and/or Communication Restriction. The list will be grouped by Site, then OT status.  It will display the patient's last appointment date and type, next appointment date and type, and last history date and type, along with the home phone number.   If the patient's name is red, that means he or she has been marked as "No Mail", and will not appear on the export list. Select "Yes" for the parameter "Display Current Hearing Aids" to see a list of the patient's current aids. 


    Once you have the list filtered as you wish, you can view it in a detail or export list format by changing the Report View parameter.

    Opportunity Tracking Report

    This report presents a  summary of appointment and associated hearing aid sales for the selected date range. It can be used to track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and closure rates of appointments. 


    The default date range will show the current month to date, but can be changed to whatever Starting and Ending Appointment Date you wish.  All Providers will be shown by default. The default grouping is first by Provider, then by Site - it can be changed to show Site first, then Provider.  Optional parameters allow for further filtering by Appointment Type, Provider, and Site.


    Below is an explanation of the various columns of the Opportunity Tracking Report:

    • Total Appts - the number of appointments in the selected period, that do not have a status of "No Show", "Cancelled" or "Rescheduled".
    • Patients Seen - the distinct number of patients seen in the period.
    • New Patients - the number of patients who's first ever appointment was in the selected period.
    • Previous Patients - number of "returning" patients (did not have a first appointment in the period).
    • Compn Rate - Companion (3rd Party) Rate - the number of appointments with a status of "Attended Companion" divided by the total number of appointments.
    • Opportunities - the number of patients seen who were designated as Opportunities at the time the appointment was made.  This would include any appointment with an OT status of "2 Ears Aidable","1 Ear Aidable", "Re-sell 2 Ears", "Re-sell 1 Ear", "Tested Not Sold", and "Tested Not Sold 1 Ear".
    • Aidable Ears - the number of "ears" that were designated as Opportunities at the time the appointment was made.  OT statuses of "2 Ears Aidable", "Re-sell 2 Ears", and "Tested Not Sold" would count as 2.  "1 Ear Aidable", "Re-sell 1 Ear", and "Tested Not Sold 1 Ear" would count as 1.  Any other OT status would count as 0.
    • Units Sold - the number of hearing aids sold associated with the appointments in the period.  When a POS Invoice is created, it is automatically linked with the last appointment the patient had.  If that invoice contains a hearing aid item (not an earmold or accessory, and is finalized, it is counted here. Credit memos are not counted in the Units Sold total.  
    • Patients Sold - the distinct number of patients who had appointments in the period and also purchased hearing aids.
    • Binaural Sales - A sale is counted as a Binaural Sale if more than one hearing aid was sold on the POS Invoice associated with the appointment.
    • Binaural % - the number of Binaural Sales divided by Patients Sold.
    • Closure rate - the number of Patients Sold divided by the number of Opportunities.
    • Companion Closure Rt - Companion (3rd Party) Closure Rate - the number of Patients Sold divided by the number of Opportunities who attended with a Companion.
    • Closure by Ears - the number of Units Sold divided by the number of Aidable Ears.
    • HA Amt Billed - the dollar amount charged on POS Invoices associated with the appointments in the period. This includes any discounts applied immediately after the hearing aid line item on the invoice. The discount amount is displayed in the details description. 


    If “Show Details” is changed to “Yes”, the details of each appointment and associated hearing aid sale (if there is one) will be shown.  The patient name, date, opportunity status (1 or 2 ear), type and status of each appointment with that source will be displayed.  If a sale was made, the invoice date, and item detail charges will be shown.  The Net Amount is the unit price of the hearing less any discount line immediately after it.
