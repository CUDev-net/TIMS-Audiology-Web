    Basic Structure

    The basic structure of TIMS for Audiology is composed of Links, Trees and Panels. The mouse is used as the primary source of motion, moving between links, panels, and trees.

     Privacy Mode

    • The Privacy Mode icon is available in any link in TIMS.
    • When enabled, all panels and lists will only display the patient's initials instead of their full name. In the Patient Summary Panel, the patient's photo will also be hidden.
    • Hover the cursor over the patient's initials in any tree, to view their date of birth.
    • When Privacy Mode is enabled, the icon will display in red.  
    • To disable and return to regular display mode, click the icon again.
