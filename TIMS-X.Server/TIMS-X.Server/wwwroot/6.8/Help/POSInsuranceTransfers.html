    POS Insurance Transfers

    The POS Transfer process offers a way to enter a hearing aid sale as a POS Invoice, and also bill the charges to an insurance payer without double-counting the sales.  The sale is categorized as a "Hearing Aid" sale, instead of a "Service" sale.  An Insurance Transfer item is used to subtract a fixed amount from the invoice that will be billed to insurance as a claim.  Once a transfer amount has been recorded in the POS Module, it will also be recorded automatically as a claim transaction, in order to balance the entire transaction and maintain the correct total sales figures. See POS Insurance Transfers for details on entering the initial transfer amount, and POS Insurance Transfer Workflow for a workflow illustration.


    When a patient has finalized a POS Insurance Transfer(s), the corresponding appointment date in the Patient Tree will be in bold black font. Highlight the appointment and you will see the transactions that have been automatically created. The Transaction type will display "New-pos".

    In the example above, the items listed are the sales items that the transfers were applied to.  If the transactions needs to be changed at all, click the Edit button.  The Claim Transaction screen will popup.  

    This will be identical to the regular Claim Transaction screen, except that the CPT and Diagnosis Codes, Unit Price, Units and Charges fields will be automatically filled in with the transferred amount.  The units can be changed, but not the Unit Price or total Charges.  The CPT and Diagnosis Codes are the ones selected at the time the transfer was recorded in POS - these can be changed here if necessary.  Add any additional information the same way as described in Adding Transaction Lines.  Click OK when finished.  Additional lines can be added if necessary. After all lines have been entered, click Save or Cancel to discard your changes.

    Combine Transfers

    Two Insurance Transfer lines can be combined into one if necessary.  For example, 2 hearing aid lines may need to be combined into one, in order to use a binaural CPT code.  

    To combine two lines, follow this procedure:

    1. Highlight both "New-pos" lines by holding down the Ctrl key and clicking on each line. The two lines must have the same cpt code.

     

    1. Click the Combine button .
    2. A message will remind you to choose a binaural CPT Code.

    1. There will now be one transaction line, replacing the two original ones.  The total charges will be equal to the sum of the two original lines.  Edit the line to choose an appropriate binaural CPT Code.

    Reversals

    If an insurance transfer has been created by mistake, it can be removed and a reversal transaction created. For example, the assumed insurance coverage didn't exist, or the claim cannot be billed for whatever reason. If this is the case, highlight the transfer line and click the Delete button . A message will popup confirming that you wish to delete and create a corresponding reversal in POS.  (the reversal is necessary so that sales totals will balance).  

    Click Yes to delete the line, or No to cancel.  The transaction line will be deleted and a new POS Insurance Transfer Reversal will saved and finalized for the same total amount.
