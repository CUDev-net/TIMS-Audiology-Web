    POS Reports

    Reports detailing POS information appear in several different folders under the main Reports folder. For a better description on all other reports and how to manipulate them please refer to the Reports section.


    Reports/Accounts Receivable

    1. Payments Received - shows a list of payments received by date. Includes payments entered in the POS module and in the Adjudication link.


    Reports/Sales

    1. Billable Items List - A list of all active billable items and their prices that can be used for POS transactions and exported to QuickBooks.  Includes Battery Sizes, CPT Codes, HA Models, and POS Items.
    2. Duplicate Item Names - A list of all duplicate item names in TIMS, so that they can be edited before exporting to QuickBooks.
    3. Hearing Aid Sales and Returns - Hearing Aid sales invoiced through POS.  Shows sales separated from returns (credit memos) with calculated ASP and Return Rate. Detail and Chart view, grouped by Manufacturer, Provider, HAType or HA Style.  Includes a parameter to view both sales and returns, sales only, or returns only.
    4. POS Transaction Report - Details of all POS transactions within the date range specified. Grouped by Site and finalized status.
    5. Refund Report - List of Refund Checks entered in specified Date Range.
    6. Sales by Item Summary - Summary of all items sold for the specified date range.  Includes sales from POS module and Claims.
    7. Sales by Referral Summary - Summary of all items sold from each Referral Source for the specified date range. Includes sales from POS module and Claims, and the percentage of total sales.  Uses the Referring Physician from the Patient details.
    8. Sales Tax Report - Summary and details of Sales Tax charged through POS in the selected date range.
    9. Unbilled Items - A list of Battery Club transactions, Hearing Aids, Payable Repairs that have not yet been invoiced in the POS module.  Also include Claim Transactions that have not yet been generated into a Claim.


    Reports/Selected - accessed through Documents button on POS Document screen.


    1. Bill of Sale (POS) - a printable receipt of the finalized invoice.
    2. Invoice History (POS) - Shows details of selected finalized invoice, plus all payments and credits applied to it. Displays remaining TIMS balance.
    3. Purchase Agreement/HA Contract - customized documents in contract form listing the details of a hearing aid purchase, deposits, warranty, etc.  Contact TIMS Support to incorporate your existing Purchase Agreement into TIMS.
