    Patient Details

    The Patient detail screen contains the office management information that pertains to the patient. From the Patient Summary panel, click the Edit Patient icon to display and edit details.

    Patient Information


    The Patient Details screen contains demographic information about the patient.  To access this information, click in the field. Information can be typed as text or click on the down arrow or calendar icon where available. The down arrow will show a list of available field choices and the calendar icon will popup a date picker.

           


    Fields highlighted in yellow are required fields.   

    Note: Beginning in version 6.07, the practice can customize which fields should be required.  See Practice Setup for details.

    Chart/Acct#

    An optional unique number assigned by the provider.  Up to 35 alpha-numeric characters can be entered. Depending on practice settings, this number can be automatically generated and also set to read only.

    Site

    The default office site for this patient. *Required field. Choose site by typing the first letter or click on the down arrow and choose from the site list. Setup site choices in the Setup Link.

    Seen By

    The default provider for this patient. If entered, this default will be used for any new Appointments, HA History, History,  or POS document created for the patient.

    Inactive

    Check this box if you no longer wish to work with this patient.  The patient and his or her data will not be removed from the database. A patient cannot be inactivated if there are outstanding appointments, claims, orders, repairs, or loaners.

    Deceased

    Check this box if the patient is deceased. Patients marked in this way are automatically filtered out of standard mailing label and mail merge reports.

    Date of Death

    If Deceased is checked, the Date of Death must also be filled out if an insurance claim is submitted for the patient.

    Digital Intake

    Click here to generate a message to the patient (Email and/or Text).  

    The message will contain a link for the patient to click on.  This will take them to the online digital intake form where they can submit their demographic information. See Digital Intake Form for more information.

    Name


      Salutation, First, Initial, Last

    The salutation, first name, middle initial and last name of the patient.  *First and Last name are required fields. 

    Note:If the patient uses a different name for insurance purposes, it can be entered

    in the Insurance Coverage section.  Choose Self-Other as the Relationship, and enter the legal name used for insurance.

    Address

    The primary mailing address of the patient.

    County

    If desired, the patient's county can be selected here.  Counties can be setup in Setup - Patient Setup.  This field will only be visible if at least one county has

    been entered in Setup.

    Secondary Address

    If the patient has a secondary address (such as a vacation address), it can be entered here.  Check the Use Secondary Address box to display this address on the Patient Summary panel or to use in mailings.  Uncheck the box to revert to using the primary address.

    Primary Phone

    Select the phone number to be used as the Primary contact number: Home, Work, Mobile, or Other.

      Home

    The home telephone number, including area code, at which the patient may be contacted.

      Work

    The work number and extension, including area code.

      Mobile

    Cell or mobile phone number, including area code.  Required if the patient is to receive appointment text notifications or for text conversations.

      Other

    Additional phone number, if needed. Enter an extension if needed.

    Email

    The patient's email address. Required if the patient is to receive appointment email notifications, or to email directly from TIMS.

    Birth date

    The patient's date of birth. The date of birth can be typed in or click on the calendar icon and choose from the calendar. Dates must be entered as MM/DD/YYYY.

    Gender

    The gender of the patient. The single letter abbreviation can be typed in or click on the down arrow and choose the patient's gender. (Female, Male, Unknown, Trans, GNC, or Other)

    SSN

    The Social Security Number (SSN) of the patient. (not required for claims) The visibility of the number can be toggled off and on by clicking the "eye" icon. Default is off.

    Name & Address for Invoicing

    If a different and and/or address should be used as a default Bill To Address for POS transactions and statements, it can be entered here.  Check the box and a details window will popup.  Enter the name and address of the default POS Bill To.

       

    Appt Authorizations

    If the Appointment Authorization module has been enabled, this is where you can view, add, and edit authorizations. Click on the plus sign to add new or double-click on existing line to edit. 

     

    The authorization details will popup.  

     

    Enter the Name, number of visits authorized and  expiration date if applicable. Check the "Show Inactive" box to display inactive (expired) authorizations.  See Appointment Authorizations for more details.

    Alternate Contact

    Free form field for additional patient contact information.

    Alternate Phone

    The phone number of the alternate contact person.

    Primary care Physician

    The name of the physician who provides the patient's primary care.

    Referring Physician

    The name of the physician who referred the patient to your office. (This physician will be the default one sent with claims, if needed)

    Responsible Party

    The name of the parent/guardian or other responsible party for the patient.

    It can also be used for QuickBooks® export, if you wish another name to be entered as a "c/o" underneath the patient name in the QuickBooks Bill To address. 

     

    QB Customer Type

    Used only for QuickBooks export.  If you wish to sync a Customer Type to QuickBooks, it can be selected here.  Available choices are the same as Patient Types.

    Language

    The patient's primary language. If Appointment Notifications are used, templates can be created that match the available languages.

    Race

    The patient's Race.

    Ethnicity

    The patient's Ethnicity.

    Marketing

    The reason that the patient first decided to visit your practice.  The marketing source can be selected when the patient is first added to the database, or added later.  The first appointment made for the patient will use this marketing source as a default.

    Patient Types

    The type of patient this is. Click on the button to display the choices.  You can

    check multiple boxes, if needed.  Additional patient type choices can be added in

    the Patient Setup.

    Marital Status

    The marital status of the patient. The marital status can be entered by typing the first letter or click on the down arrow and choose from the marital status list. (not required for claims)

    Employment Status

    The employment status of the patient. The employment status can be entered by typing the first letter or click on the down arrow and choose from the employment status list. (not required for claims)

    Student Status

    The student status of the patient. The student status can be entered by typing the first letter or click on the down arrow and choose from the student status list. (not required for claims)

    Patient Status

    The status of the patient. Choose status by typing the first letter or click on the down arrow and choose from the status list. 

    Release Signature

    Check this box and fill in the date if the patient has signed a release statement allowing medical information to be shared with the insurance payer.

    Assignment of Benefits

    A check mark used to indicate whether the patient has a signed form authorizing the payer to pay the provider, and whether the provider accepts such a payment arrangement. Check this box and fill in the date the form was signed.

    Privacy Agreement

    The date the privacy statement was signed.

    Consent

    The date the consent statement was signed.

    Marketing Authorization

    Check this box and fill in the date if the patient has signed a release statement allowing marketing materials to sent from the authorized parties selected below.

      Authorizations

    The parties which have been authorized to market to the patient.  Authorized parties can be added in Patient Setup.

    Release of Information

    Check this box and fill in the date if the patient has signed a release statement allowing medical information to be shared with another party.

      Authorized Parties

    Type in the name of other parties authorized to receive medical information about the patient.

    Appt Reminder and Communication Restrictions

    If the patient has any restrictions on how they are to be communicated with, enter

    them here.  Click on the Select button to display the restriction choices. You can check multiple boxes, if needed.  Additional restriction choices can be added in Patient Setup. Certain restrictions are protected are used as components of other TIMS functions.

    • No Mail - used to filter the patient from receiving various marketing letters and/or emails.
    • No Marketing - Used for Opportunity Tracking. If checked, a patient will be flagged as No Marketing, regardless of the condition of either/both ears (i.e. whether there is hearing loss and/or outdated hearing aids for either ear).
    • No Marketing 1 Ear - Used for Opportunity Tracking. If checked, a patient will be flagged as No Marketing, regardless of the condition of either/both ears (i.e. whether there is hearing loss and/or outdated hearing aids for either ear). For example, a patient may have a hearing loss in both ears but chooses to purchase only 1 hearing aid.  If they wish not to be marketed to for the un-aided ear, check this box.
    • No Email Notifications - Used in Appointment Notifications. Check this box to specifically exclude the patient from receiving text notifications.
    • No Text Notifications - Used in Appointment Notifications. Check this box to specifically exclude the patient from receiving text notifications.
    • No Voice Notifications - Used in Appointment Notifications. Check this box to specifically exclude the patient from receiving text notifications.

    Notes

    A free form field for miscellaneous notes about the patient. Allows input of up to 10,000 characters. 

    Custom Fields


      Custom Date 1 & 2

    Custom dates that can display a date for whatever purpose the practice needs. Set up in Patient Setup - Field Descriptions.

      Custom Field 1 & 2

    Custom field that can display custom text  for whatever purpose the practice needs. Set up in Patient Setup - Field Descriptions.

      Additional Custom Fields

    If the practice has been setup to allow additional Custom Fields, this button will be available. 

     

    Click to enter information for the custom fields that have been previously setup in Patient Setup - Custom Fields.

    Insurance

    Click this button to display the Insurance Details for the patient.  

     

    This is where you can add or edit subscriber information for up to five insurance payers.  See Insurance Coverage for details.

    Clone Patient

    To quickly create a new patient based on the demographics for the current one, click the Clone Patient button . A new patient details screen will popup with the Last Name, Address, phone numbers, and physicians copied from the original one.  Enter a new first name, initial, and Date of Birth and click OK to save the new patient.
