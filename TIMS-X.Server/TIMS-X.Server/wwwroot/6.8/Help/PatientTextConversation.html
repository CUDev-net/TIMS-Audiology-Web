    Patient Text Conversation

    Starting in version 6.07, you can now have a two-way text conversation with a patient directly from the Patient Summary Panel.  To use this feature, the practice must be enrolled in Automated Appointment Notification service. Contact TIMS Support at 800-763-0308 for more information. The practice must be replicated - fees applicable.


    1. With the desired patient selected, click the Conversations button on the Patient Summary Panel.

    1. The Conversations screen will popup.  Mobile number will default to what has been entered in the patient's details. 

    1. Type the message to be sent, or access a Scripted Note by right-clicking on the Compose Message field. Scripted notes are user-specific and must first be setup under the Communications note type to be available here. See Scripted Notes setup.

    1. Click the Send button .  Confirm the message to be sent is correct, then click the Confirm Send button . Otherwise, click Cancel to return and edit the message.
    2. After sending, the message will appear in the conversation history section. If the patient replies, their response will be shown below. 

             

      1. The patient can reply with an image file, if needed. (for example, picture of insurance card).  If an image is received, it can be saved to the patient record by right-clicking on it and choosing Save Image. 

    The TIMS Document Capture System screen will popup. Choose a Document Type for the image and save. The saved image will now appear in the patient's Activity Panel under Patient Documents, then the Document Type selected.

    1. The Conversations window can be left open and minimized while you are working in other areas of TIMS.  
      1. If a reply from the patient comes in while the window is minimized, you will see the TIMS icon on the Windows task bar highlighted and blinking.  Click on it to bring the Conversations window back up.
      2. If a reply is received when the window is closed, and alert will popup if the provider has alerts turned on. Choose Snooze to view later, or View to reopen the Conversations window.

    1. Conversations with more than one patient can be conducted at the same time.  Each conversation will be shown in its own tab in the Conversations window.  

    1. After the Conversations window is closed, previous conversations can be viewed and re-started from the Patient Activity panel.  Double-click on View Conversation under Conversations to re-open the Conversations window. Messages from all users to the patient will be shown in the same window.

      1. If the patient has many messages extending back more than 1 month, all of them may not be loaded in the Conversations window by default.  Click the Load All link to display all messages.

    1. Conversations for all patients can be viewed in the Conversations Center.
