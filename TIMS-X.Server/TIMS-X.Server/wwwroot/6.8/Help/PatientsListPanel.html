    Patients List Panel

    • The Patient Tree displays the names of the 20 most recently accessed patients. 
    • Recent patient names are stored per user so one user’s access will not change another user’s tree.
    • A patient’s name will move to the top of the tree when their name is double-clicked. 
    • A patient’s name will also move to the top of the tree when a patient appointment created, clicked-on, or edited. 
    • An arrow symbol   to the left of the name indicates that the patient has a recent or future appointment. Click the arrow to expand the tree and view the appointment date and time.   Double-click on the date to view the appointment details.

    • The gender symbol next to the name indicates whether the patient is male or female. A name in gray font indicates an inactive patient.
    • The tree can be collapsed and hidden, if more room on the screen is needed to display the calendar.  Click on the thumbtack icon to un-pin the tree from the view.  This will collapse the tree to the left, and make it a sliding view.  The tree can be viewed again by clicking on the label "Patients". To re-pin (dock) the tree, click the sideways thumbtack icon .
    • Hovering the cursor over the name will display the birth date of the patient.

    Patient Search


    • The Patient Search function allows a user to search the entire TIMS database for a patient that is not currently on the patient tree.
    • At least two letters or numbers must be entered in order to enable the Magnifying Glass search button and list the search criteria options.
    • Searches can be made for a patient based on 
      • Last Name 
      • First Name 
      • Birth Date 
      • Social Security Number
      • Phone Number 
      • Hearing Aid Serial Number
      • Account Number
      • Hearing Aid PO Number
      • Repair Number
      • Address Line1
      • City
      • Zip Code
      • POS Document number
    • There is a check box to include Inactive patients.
    • Including Inactive patients is useful to ensure duplicate patients are not created.
    • Click on the Magnifying Glass search icon or press the Enter key to initiate the search.  Search results are displayed at the bottom of the panel.

    • To exit the Search screen clear search box of all entered letters or numbers.

    Add a New Patient

    If the patient you are searching for is not in the database, you can add the patient by clicking the Add New Patient button.

    The Add New Patient screen will popup.  Enter as much information as possible, to save time later.  The fields highlighted in yellow are required, and are customizable in Practice setup. A Marketing Source may also be required, depending on the practice rules. After entering all available information, click OK.  The new patient's name will now appear at the top of the Patient Tree.
