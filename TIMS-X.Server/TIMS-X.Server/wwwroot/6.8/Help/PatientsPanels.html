    Patients Panels

    • The Patients and Tasks Panels are the only panels on the left side of the screen.
    • The Patients Panel is available on the Appointments, History, Hearing Aid History,Communications and Claims links. 
    • Icons and actions are different for each page where the Patients Panel is available.

    Add New Patient

    • Click the Quick Add icon Patient icon to the Add New Patient window.
    • When a new patient is added that patient name will appear at the top of the list.

    Find Patients

    • The Patient Search function allows a user to search for a patient that is not currently in the list of 20. 
    • Enter two letters or two numbers to open the Patient Search Panel.

    Patient Search Panel

    • Searches can be made for: 

    • A minimum of 2 characters must be entered for a search.
    • There is a check box to include Inactive patients.
    • Including Inactive patients in a search for a patient name is useful to ensure duplicate patients are not created.
    • Click on the Magnifying Glass search button or press the enter key to initiate the search.
    • Search results are displayed at the bottom of the panel.  Double click on the desired patient name to see their details and move them to the top of the patient list.

    • To exit the Patient Search Panel clear search box of all entered letters or numbers.
