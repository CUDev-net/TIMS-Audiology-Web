  1. Introduction

    System Requirements

    TIMS Database Minimum System Requirements

    IBM compatible PC with the following:

    • Windows 10 (32 bit or 64 bit)
    • Intel Quad-Core or higher
    • 16 GB RAM
    • 10 GB** free hard drive space (**May require additional space to allow for database growth)
    • 17" SVGA Compatible Monitor or higher
    • Keyboard & Mouse
    • Broadband Internet connection


    Client Minimum System Requirements

    IBM compatible PC with the following:

    • Windows 10 (32 bit or 64 bit)
    • Intel Quad-Core or higher
    • 8 GB RAM
    • 5 GB free hard drive space
    • 17" SVGA Compatible Monitor or higher
    • Keyboard & Mouse
    • Broadband Internet connection


    Local Area Network Requirements

    • TCP/IP Ethernet Network 100MBPS network
    • Wi-Fi connections not recommended due to poor bandwidth quality and reliability
    • Multi-location practices: A replicated copy of TIMS should be setup on one PC at each remote location using TIMS SQL replication services.
    • TIMS Support does not offer any setup, support, or troubleshooting of Ethernet or Wi-Fi connections. Network performance is the responsibility of the customer.
    • TIMS does not support Terminal Services (Remote Desktop) or thin clients


    Requirements for Additional Features

    • Microsoft SQL Server 2016 or newer (contact TIMS support for details )
    • QuickBooks® 2021 (or higher) Pro or Premier
    • NOAH® 4.14 or higher with NOAH Engine License.  Requires a minimum of 5 GB free Hard Drive space.
    • Web Camera for capturing patient pictures
    • Scanner with TWAIN driver for uploading scanned documents
    • Topaz® signature pad for capturing signatures on reports


    HIPAA/HITECH Compliancy

    In order to maintain compliancy for your organization we suggest that all devices (laptops, workstations, servers) that operate or house TIMS should utilize full-disk encryption (BitLocker / Sophos Safeguard). This will help ensure that any loss or theft of hardware will not result in a HIPAA / HITECH violation and minimize possible impacts if such loss or theft does occur. 


    Please contact support for additional details or recommended requirements at 800-763-0308.
