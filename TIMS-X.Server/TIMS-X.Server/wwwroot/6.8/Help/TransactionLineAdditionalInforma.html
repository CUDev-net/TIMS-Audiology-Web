    Transaction Line Additional Information

    The Transaction Line Additional Details are the extra details of each Transaction Line that are specific to just that line. Certain payers may require some of the information in this screen.  To access the Additional Details, select the line and click the Edit button .  

    From the Claim Transaction details screen, click the Additional Information button .


    The Additional Claim Line Information screen will appear.


    Line Note (Included with Claim)

    Descriptive notes to be included with the claim. Some insurance companies may require this information for certain procedures. The maximum number of characters that can be entered for each transaction line is 80.

    Notes (Internal Use)

    Notes that will not be sent to the insurance company, but for internal use only. These may include adjudication notes previously received from the payer, if this transaction has been submitted before.

    Payments

    The amount(s) paid by the primary payer, secondary payer, and/or patient for this procedure.

    Note:  If this is a resubmitted transaction, this amount will be carried over from the Adjudication link, if it was entered there. This field cannot be edited in this screen.

    Adjustments

    The amount(s) adjustment by the primary payer, secondary payer, and/or patient for this procedure.

    Note:  If this is a resubmitted transaction, this amount will be carried over from the Adjudication link, if it was entered there. This field cannot be edited in this screen.

    Balance of Claim

    The balance due on the procedure, after any payments and/or adjustments have been applied.
