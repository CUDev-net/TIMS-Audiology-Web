    Viewing Appointment Requests

    After appointment requests have been submitted and replication has occurred, they will be visible in TIMS Appointments. This is where they will be reviewed, assigned to the correct patient (new or existing), appointment type, date, time and duration.  The patient then needs to be contacted to either confirm the final details of their appointment and/or information, or reschedule.


    Appointment requests will automatically be added to the schedule using the default patient "Online Request" and the default appointment type "Online". They will be added for a one hour duration at the time requested by the patient. There may be some conflicts on the schedule - this could happen if multiple patients requested the same slot at the same time, or an appointment was made in TIMS while the patient was requesting one online.  These conflicts will need to be resolved by moving the appointments involved.


    Patient Appointment Requests Panel

    • This panel will be visible in the Appointments link once Online Patient Scheduling has been setup for the practice. Initially, the panel will be hidden and the tab for it will be in the lower left corner of the screen.

    • Click on the tab to expand the panel.  If it will be used frequently, pin the panel so that it stays expanded.

    • Click the Build List button to generate a list of all the outstanding online appointment requests.

    • The list generated will show the information entered online for the requested appointments. The list will be ordered by requested appointment date and time, with the most recent first.

    Appointment Grid

    • The associated appointments will also appear on the appointment grid, assigned to patient Online Request.  The information entered by the patient will visible in the Notes field of the appointment.
